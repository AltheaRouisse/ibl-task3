MY HOBBIES

PIANO

I started playing the piano when I was 6 years old. To be honest, piano was kinda boring when I was a small kid. But I realized that you can make someone happy by playing the piano for them.

GUITAR

I started playing the guitar because I was inspired by a cover of my favorite song that I found on youtube. Now, guitar is my most favorite instrument. I play it whenever I want to express my feelings.

UKULELE

My favorite artist BILLIE EILISH inspired me to start playing the ukulele. Since I play the guitar, it didn't took me long to learn it. The quiet and beautiful sounds of it's strings always makes me calm and gives me time to relax.
